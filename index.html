Just one last thing!

by Kevin Kimble


Kevin has just started his Geometry homework. His professor requested that the class find real life examples of the following:


A square, rectangle, pentagon, triangle and a circle.


Easy enough right? Wrong! “Who is this so hard to do. It’s the last assignment and I”m struggling to get it done”


Kevin reached out to his friends and family and asked that everyone send over a photo go what they could find.


His Mom, always willing to help, was the first to reply. She sent over a pretty Rectangle in the form of shoe box that she was hanging on to.


“Thanks mom!” Kevin replied, eager to snap a photo to submit for his assignment.


His next lifeline came from his best friend Heather who sent over a Circles in the form of a cereal bowl she’d used for breakfast.


“Geez, do you think you could have washed the bowl out first?” Kevin whined.
